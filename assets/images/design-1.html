Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our courses, placements, and more

What is the duration of the courses?

Our courses typically range from 4 to 12 months, depending on the specialization. Each course is designed to provide in-depth knowledge and hands-on experience with industry-relevant projects.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive career support including resume building, interview preparation, portfolio reviews, and direct connections with our hiring partners. Our placement success rate is over 85% within 6 months of course completion.

Are the courses suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our courses are designed for learners at all levels. We start with fundamentals and gradually progress to advanced topics. No prior experience is required, just enthusiasm to learn and grow.

What kind of projects will I work on?

You'll work on 12-25 real-world projects depending on your course, including client simulations, industry case studies, and portfolio pieces. Projects range from individual assignments to collaborative team projects that mirror actual workplace scenarios.

Do I get a certificate upon completion?

Yes, you'll receive an industry-recognized certificate upon successful completion of your course. Our certificates are valued by employers and can be verified online. We also provide LinkedIn skill badges and recommendations.

What is the class schedule and format?

We offer flexible learning options including live online classes, recorded sessions, and hybrid formats. Classes are typically held 3-4 times per week in the evenings, making it perfect for working professionals and students.
